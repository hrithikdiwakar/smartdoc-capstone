About Us

Welcome to SmartDoc

Whenever a person is affected with any kind of disease, visiting the doctor is a primary necessity. But, it is tiresome and expensive to go to hospital all the time. People living far away from hospitals do not wish to travel long distances every time they experience any kind of illness. Thanks to machine learning, which has made the job of disease prediction easier. Our application predicts diseases based on symptoms provided by the user. The diseases that can be predicted are Pneumonia , Malaria and Hepatitis.

We are a team of four members, doing SmartDoc as part of our final year capstone project.The motivation and scope of the project lie in advancing the healthcare industry using predictive analysis. In the biomedical and healthcare communities, reliable diagnosis plays a critical role in determining the patient's disease risk. The prediction gives the benefits of early disease detection.
